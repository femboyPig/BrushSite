Why Brush Client?

This client is made in a rather vanilla way,

Without creating any custom interfaces that would affect its performance.

It has most of the useful features in pvp, as well as in the visual part of the game.

The client is updated, and will continue to update to the most current versions of the game.

How Install?

1. Download .jar from modrinth

2. Move the .jar to .minecraft/mods

3. Done! You can play with pleasure!

Description

Brush Client is a simple, optimized pvp client with more than 30+ features that are responsible for both the visual part of the game and the mechanics of the game itself.

visual part of the game, as well as the mechanics of the game itself. There are 4 categories of functions in the client: Combat, Render, Misc, HUD

Each of which has at least 5 functions that will diversify your game and help you in pvp!

Also our mod supports Discord RPC and is an open source project to gain your trust in us!

Brush Client will support the newest and most up to date versions of the game to keep you always with us!
